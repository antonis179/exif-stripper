<b>Exif Stripper removes private information from your photos stored as image metadata (exif) so you can share them safely.
</b>

Still in the early stages of development.
The UI is still under construction.

<b>Current Features:
</b>
• Remove metadata from an image
• Save or Share the stripped images
• Share an image to the app directly from your gallery

<b>Under development:
</b>
• Option to share image to the app for metadata removal
• Option to remove a single attribute instead of all metadata
• Option to remove metadata from multiple images at once
• Option to monitor specific folders and automatically remove image metadata as new images are taken

If you have a feature idea you would like to see in the app please let me know.
